# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Stonewright is built for careful, constructive collaboration. Contributors are
expected to be respectful, patient, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming, professional language.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give feedback about code, design, and documentation without personal attacks.
- Keep security reports private until maintainers have reviewed them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive comments that derail issues, reviews, or discussions.
- Public disclosure of unpatched security vulnerabilities.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation
for behavior that violates this code. Reports can be sent privately to the
project maintainer.
