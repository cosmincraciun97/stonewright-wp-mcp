## Purpose
To add a new slide, in the Content tab, under the Slides section, click the Add Item button. You can also create the exact copy of the existing slide by clicking the icon. Click the delete icon to delete a slide.

## Use this when
- What is the Carousel widget
- The Carousel widget allows you to use nested elements within the carousel items
- This means you can design and structure each item within the carousel with different elements, allowing for a unique and customized presentation
- Also, the widget gives you the flexibility to create carousels that match your specific needs and preferences
