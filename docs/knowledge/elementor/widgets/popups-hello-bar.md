## Purpose
A Hello Bar is a horizontal notification banner that displays at the top of your website to capture visitor attention and drive engagement. It is commonly used for announcements, promotions, or calls-to-action without interrupting the main content experience.

## Use this when
- You want to announce time-sensitive offers or promotions across your site
- You need a non-intrusive way to highlight important messages
- You're promoting newsletter signups or special events
- You want higher visibility than standard page elements but less disruptive than popups
- You're testing engagement strategies without full-page takeovers

## Settings highlights
- Position — set bar to display at top or bottom of viewport
- Display Conditions — control which pages, devices, or user segments see the bar
- Animation — choose entrance effects and transitions
- Close Button — enable visitor dismissal options
- Responsive Design — adjust styling for mobile, tablet, and desktop views
- Triggers — set timing (on page load, after delay, on scroll)
- Background & Typography — customize colors, fonts, and spacing
- Call-to-Action — add buttons or links with custom actions

## Limits / gotchas
- Excessive Hello Bars may reduce effectiveness through visitor fatigue
- Mobile spacing requires careful testing to avoid covering critical content
- Dismissal persistence varies based on cookie/session settings
- Display condition complexity can inadvertently hide bars from intended audiences
