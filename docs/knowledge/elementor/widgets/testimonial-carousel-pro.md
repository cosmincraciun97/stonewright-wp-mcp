## Purpose
Shows a list of all the items in the slide. You can drag and drop them to change their order. Click on the Add Item button to add another item to the list.Click the icon to copy a slide.Click the icon to delete a slide.In the Content field, enter the text of the testimonial.In the Image field, upload or choose an image from the media library.In the Name field, enter the customer’s name.In the Title field, enter the customer’s title. Skin

## Use this when
- In Elementor Editor, click +
- All available widgets are displayed
- Click or drag the widget to the canvas
- For more information, see Add elements to a page
