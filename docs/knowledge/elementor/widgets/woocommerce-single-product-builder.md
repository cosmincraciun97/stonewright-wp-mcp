## Purpose
Enables the design of dynamic Single Product templates in WooCommerce using dedicated Elementor Pro widgets.

## Use this when
- Designing a custom layout for individual product listings across the shop.

## Settings highlights
- Templates > Theme Builder > Single Product (navigation path)
- Product Title widget
- Product Images / Product Gallery widget
- Product Price widget
- Add to Cart widget
- Product Tabs widget
- Product Meta widget
- Product Related products widget

## Limits / gotchas
- Dynamic widgets display mock placeholders in the editor if no preview product is selected in settings.
- Elementor Pro and WooCommerce are mandatory.
