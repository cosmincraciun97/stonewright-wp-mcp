## Purpose
The Custom Add To Cart widget enables WooCommerce product purchases directly within Elementor pages. This Pro-exclusive widget displays product quantity selectors and add-to-cart buttons with customizable styling, allowing merchants to showcase individual products with full purchasing functionality without redirecting to standard product pages.

## Use this when
- Creating custom product display pages with integrated purchasing options
- Building promotional landing pages featuring specific WooCommerce items
- Designing product showcase sections with immediate checkout capability
- Customizing product presentation beyond default WooCommerce templates
- Wanting to maintain consistent branding while preserving e-commerce functionality

## Settings highlights
- Product selection dropdown to choose which item to display
- Quantity selector customization and visibility controls
- Button text, color, and styling options for add-to-cart action
- Price display toggle and formatting preferences
- Stock status indicator settings and low-stock warnings
- Product image carousel or gallery integration controls
- Responsive behavior adjustments for mobile/tablet viewports
- Form field layout arrangement (horizontal/vertical options)

## Limits / gotchas
- Requires Elementor Pro subscription to access widget functionality
- Only compatible with active WooCommerce installations and product data
- Advanced customization may require familiarity with WooCommerce product settings
- Some styling options depend on active theme compatibility with Elementor
